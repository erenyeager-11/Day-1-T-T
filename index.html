a little world made just for you
hey
you.

You are the kind of thing
that makes ordinary days feel like they were written by someone who really cared.

These days without you feel like a song missing its best part.
So here is a small pocket universe I made for you to wander through. 🌸

look at this absolute cutie

you walked in and
I forgot how to think

You do that thing where you are just existing and somehow that alone makes the whole room feel chosen.

Honestly it is genuinely unfair. You are not even trying and yet here I am, absolutely gone for you in the most embarrassing way. 🐻

the softest things

your laugh is the sound
I rewind the most

Some sounds just land differently and yours does something to my chest that I have no good words for yet.

I keep things you say tucked away like little folded notes. They are treasures. You are a treasure. 🫶

🌙
a letter I am writing from here

I hope wherever you are
feels like a warm blanket

I hope the people around you are kind. I hope your food is good and your sleep is deep and nobody gives you any trouble.

And I hope you know that back here, someone is thinking about you so quietly and so constantly it has basically become a background process. 🌟
